Aesthetics Treatments

Botox

Botox is a chemical used for preventative anti-ageing and when injected into certain muscles in the face, will stop repeated muscle contractions which lead to unsightly lines and wrinkles.


What areas of the face?

  • Forehead
  • Cross Feet
  • Side of the eyes

Botox per treatment £250

Skin Boosters Treatments

Skin boosters are generally hyaluronic acid based solutions, but they also can contain vitamins, amino acids and anti-oxidants that can be injected into the skin to improve the appearance of the face. They help to reduce fine lines and wrinkles, leaving the skin looking more radiant. They can help reduce the appearance of dark circles as well as deeply nourishing and plumping the skin. Skin Boosters are generally known for stimulating collagen production, leaving the skin looking rejuvenated.


What areas of the face?

  • Forehead
  • Cross Feet
  • Under the eyes
  • Smoker lines
  • Décolletage
  • Neck
  • Hands

Profhilo

Profhilo is a moisturising solution containing a high concentration of Hyaluronic Acid. It is injected into the skin and works more effectively than a moisturiser that is applied on the surface of the skin.



What are the benefits of Profhilo?

  • Hydrates the skin
  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Revitalises dull and tired looking skin
  • Restores firmness to the skin

What areas of the face can Profhilo be used in?

  • Top of cheeks
  • Side of face
  • Lines around mouth
  • Nasolabial folds
  • Neck
  • Chest and décolletage

Profhilo per treatment £250

Sunekos 200

Sunekos is an injectable treatment formulated with a special combination of hyaluronic acid and amino acids. It targets specific areas of concern including dark circles under the eyes and “smokers lines” above the lips.


What are the benefits of Sunekos 200?

  • Stimulates the synthesis of collagen and elastin
  • Restores the skin's natural elasticity and volume
  • Enhances youthful and natural features of the face

What areas of the face can Sunekos 200 be used on?

  • Eye area
  • Lines around mouth
  • Cheeks
  • Neck
